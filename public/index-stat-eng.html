Art school "Khudozhka" is working in the area of additional professional art education.

The program includes a full course of study at an art school (drawing, painting, easel-composition, history of art, decorative and applied art, plein air). The optimally small groups (8 people) allows for an individual approach to every student, and also provides an opportunity for in-depth study of not only program material, but also additional topics and techniques, depending on the characteristics and desires of each student.
